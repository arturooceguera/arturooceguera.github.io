Spotify Playlist Covers

Graphic Designs




Adobe Photoshop

These are a part of a collection for a series of Spotify playlist covers. With the rise in popularity in Spotify, users are constantly looking to more flare to their playlists. Users want to share their playlists publicly throughout the web and gain many followers to follow the playlist they have created. To stand out people place artwork on the playlist cover to attract users.


These are a series of album covers for different playlists and each cover represents the genre and mood of the playlist.
