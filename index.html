Who am I?

Hello, my name is Arturo Oceguera, and I am a web developer and graphics designer from the Chicago area. I graduated from North Central College with a BAF in Interactive Media Studies and a minor in Computer Science.

I have always been passionate about computers and creating things from my imagination.

During my time at North Central College, I've had the opportunity to be involved in several programming projects, ranging from console to Graphics User Interface. I decided to combine my interests in programming and graphics design to persue a career in Web Development.
